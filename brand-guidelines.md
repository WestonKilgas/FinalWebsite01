# Château Barkalot — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence

**Brand Name:** Château Barkalot
**Archetype:** The Jester-Sage
**One-line identity:** A kindly royal herald who has spent the morning giving belly rubs.

---

## Voice & Tone

### Tone Dimensions
| Axis | Score | Direction |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | 2/5 | Casual with courtly flourishes |
| Serious ↔ Playful | 5/5 | Fully playful |
| Reserved ↔ Expressive | 4/5 | Warm and expressive |
| Modern ↔ Timeless | 4/5 | Timeless with a wink |

### Voice Rules
1. **One medieval phrase per paragraph maximum.** Sprinkle sparingly: "hark," "verily," "pull up a tankard," "the round table awaits." Never go full Ren-faire.
2. **Dog puns are earned, not stacked.** Maximum one pun per section.
3. **Second person always.** "You" and "your pup" — never "customers" or "guests."
4. **Active voice. Short sentences.** Warmth beats cleverness when they conflict.
5. **Warmth first.** Every line should feel like a welcome, not a pitch.
